Welcome to TOPSIS Model Ranking

Rank your machine learning models using the TOPSIS method. Upload a CSV file with your models, assign weights and impacts to each attribute, and get ranked results instantly!

What is TOPSIS?

TOPSIS (Technique for Order of Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution) helps identify the best option by measuring distance from the ideal and worst-case solutions.

How It Works

Upload a CSV of your models, specify weights and impacts for each attribute, and receive a ranked list of your models based on the TOPSIS method.

Get Started

Visit the Rank Models page, upload your data, and view the ranked results. You can also review past rankings in the History section.

How to Use the Website

1. Visit the Rank Models page and upload your CSV file with model data. 2. Enter the weights for each attribute from the CSV file. 3. Specify the impact of each attribute (positive or negative). 4. View the ranked results of your models in the generated table. 5. Review past rankings in the History section.
